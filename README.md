## 🎯 Why This Project Exists

Most startup founders build products based on intuition.

AI Startup Radar continuously scans GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit, Product Hunt, and ArXiv to identify emerging technology niches before they become mainstream.

The goal is to transform raw developer activity into actionable startup opportunities.
